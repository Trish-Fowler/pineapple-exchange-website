About Pineapple Exchange, LLC

Pineapple Exchange, LLC is the ownership and operating company behind our technology and creative service brands. Based on the South Carolina coast, we provide a foundation for web development, cloud infrastructure, and digital media projects, with an emphasis on reliability, clarity, and long-term client relationships.

The pineapple has long been a symbol of welcome and hospitality. We bring that same spirit to the way we manage projects, communicate with clients, and deliver solutions.

Blue Crab Bytes (DBA)

Pineapple Exchange, LLC conducts day-to-day client work under the trade name Blue Crab Bytes. Blue Crab Bytes focuses on practical, right-sized solutions for local businesses, including:

  • Website design, redesign, and content organization
  • Managed hosting and ongoing maintenance
  • Cloud and automation consulting for small teams
  • Brand-supporting assets such as logos, graphics, and simple media
